Eat more chocolate… good for health A recent study by the Indian Institute of Nutritional Sciences has revealed that eating dark chocolate daily can significantly boost heart health. Researchers credit this benefit to the high flavonoid content in dark chocolate, which improves blood vessel function and reduces inflammation. Participants who consumed 30 grams of 70% dark chocolate each day also experienced lower stress and improved mood. However, scientists warn that milk chocolate and sugary treats do not offer the same benefits, and portion control remains important. Plans are underway for a larger international study to confirm these sweet findings across different populations.
